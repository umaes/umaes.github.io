The objectives of the Association are:

  • Protection of interests of the Association members, i.e. minority shareholders of issuers from the energy sector on the territory of the Republic of Srpska, especially:
    • Hydro power plants on Trebišnjica JSC Trebinje
    • Hydro power plants on Drina JSC Višegrad
    • Hydro power plants on Vrbas JSC Mrkonjić Grad
    • Elektrokrajina JSC Banja Luka
    • Elektro Doboj JSC Doboj
    • Elektrodistribucija Pale JSC Pale
    • Mine and Thermal power plant Gacko JSC Gacko
    • Mine and Thermal power plant Ugljevik JSC Ugljevik
  • Increasing the value of the Association members assets through active contribution to the development and improvement of the capital market environment, as well as through the contribution to the development of corporate governance in the issuers individually,
  • Financial education of the Association members and all interested citizens.

The Association achieves its goals through the following activities:

  • Organizing joint action of shareholders
  • Organizing representation of members at shareholders' meetings
  • Responses through public media outlets and media
  • Organizing representation before courts and state bodies
  • Organizing the engagement of an independent auditor of the issuers business
  • Submitting initiatives and proposals to the Securities Commission, Stock Exchange and other institutions in order to improve the environment in the capital market, as well as, due to possible irregularities that may be occurred and observed, its functioning
  • Submitting initiatives and proposals to the issuers, asking for announcements and clarifications regarding important business events from their operations
  • Organizing educational and promotional activities that aim to promote standards of corporate governance and financial education
  • Cooperation with similar and related organizations at home and abroad
